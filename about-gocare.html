Our Vision

To be the leading and most preferred center of excellence in equipping learners with useful knowledge and skills.

Our Mission

To carefully and diligently equip learners with adequate skills and knowledge that help them to exceptionally stand out in the job market while delivering excellent services in the society, confidently and in a competent manner.

Core Values

Our Core Values are; Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Client satisfaction, Innovation and skill development.

GoCare Training Institute (GTI) is a Kenyan tertiary education institution founded in 2018 and since then it has trained thousands of graduates and helped them realize their dream career(s) in the health and patient care related fields. We are located in Nairobi, Kenya. The training institute is affiliated to GoCare Health Solutions Limited, a pace setter and leading provider of professional home healthcare services since 2011.

Here are some of the courses and programs we offer:

  • Community Health Assistant Level 6
  • Community Health Assistant Level 5
  • Community Health Assistant Level 4
  • Diploma in Community Health Assistant
  • Health Services Support Level 5
  • Health Services Support Level 4
  • Patient Attendant Course

We also offer a variety of short courses and workshops on topics such as first aid, CPR, and infection control.
